## Business Ideas

### 1. Rental Management Platform (Puerto Rico) — Gerald's Primary Concept
A property management and communication platform designed specifically for the Puerto Rico rental market, where no equivalent currently exists. Gerald is a real estate broker who holds leases for 1–1.5 year terms and manages tenant-landlord relationships.

**Core Features Discussed:**
- Document submission portal (tenants submit payment evidence, applications, paperwork through the platform — no direct email/phone contact)
- Automated email notices (late payment notices, formal communications sent by the platform in the correct legal format)
- Communication slots for maintenance requests (toilet broken, electrical issues) — structured, not freeform
- Vendor/contractor bidding system (plumbers, electricians submit bids; certificates of insurance and W-4s pre-uploaded)
- Landlord dashboard (see real-time status, vacancy rates, payment status)
- Tenant portal (submit documents, see listings, schedule requests)
- Listing display with real-time availability/counter-offer transparency (Gerald's idea: show 48-hour counteroffer windows publicly to create urgency)

- RentSpree: document upload only, no communication layer
